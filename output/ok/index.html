Safety Tips for Using Scissor Lifts

When operating scissor lifts in Oklahoma, always follow these essential safety guidelines:

  • Pre-Operation Inspection: Always inspect the scissor lift before use, checking for hydraulic leaks, damaged controls, or structural issues
  • Weight Limitations: Never exceed the manufacturer's specified weight capacity, including all personnel, tools, and materials
  • Platform Maintenance: Keep the platform clean and free of debris, oil, or other slip hazards
  • Overhead Awareness: Be aware of overhead obstructions including power lines, ceiling fixtures, and building structures
  • Level Operation: Only operate on level surfaces unless using a rough terrain model specifically designed for uneven ground
  • Proper Training: Ensure all operators have received proper training and certification for scissor lift operation
  • Weather Considerations: Avoid outdoor operation during high winds, storms, or extreme weather conditions
  • Manufacturer Guidelines: Follow all manufacturer guidelines and safety protocols specific to your equipment model
