Safety Considerations for Scissor Lift Operation

When operating scissor lifts in Burr Ridge, always prioritize safety with these essential guidelines:

  • Weather Awareness: Consider local Burr Ridge weather conditions, avoiding outdoor operation during high winds or storms
  • Fall Protection: Always use appropriate fall protection equipment when required by regulations or manufacturer guidelines
