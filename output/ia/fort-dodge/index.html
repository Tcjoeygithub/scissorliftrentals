Safety Considerations for Scissor Lift Operation

When operating scissor lifts in Fort Dodge, always prioritize safety with these essential guidelines:

  • Operator Training: Ensure all personnel have proper training and certification before operating equipment
  • Site Assessment: Evaluate the work area for potential hazards including uneven surfaces, overhead obstructions, and power lines
  • Equipment Inspection: Thoroughly inspect the scissor lift before each use, checking controls, safety devices, and hydraulic systems
  • Weather Awareness: Consider local Fort Dodge weather conditions, avoiding outdoor operation during high winds or storms
  • Fall Protection: Always use appropriate fall protection equipment when required by regulations or manufacturer guidelines
  • Load Management: Never exceed the manufacturer's specified weight capacity for the specific model
